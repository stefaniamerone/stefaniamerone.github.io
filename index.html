About

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at University of Oxford, I will be on the job market in 2026–27. My research interests include decision theory, behavioral economics, and microeconomic theory. I am interested in axiomatic models of choice and in what departures from expected-utility behavior reveal about the underlying preferences that generate them.

Before my PhD I completed an MPhil at Oxford. I am on the 2026–27 economics job market.
